§ 03 / Why Dubai

Why Dubai.

We started in Cancún, making souvenir tees for a resort-town economy. A decade of it.

Then we came to the UAE for a short business trip in mid-2025. A regional conflict rerouted our flights, our calendar, and eventually our entire company. We watched a country quietly, competently absorb a genuinely unsettling week — and noticed how rare that is.

We filed for residency instead of leaving. Registered a company. Named it after what we saw.

Our shirts change color in sunlight.
So did we.
