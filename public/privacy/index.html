Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

This policy explains what information Våld Labs ("we", "us") collects and how we handle it across our products, applications, audio plugins, and websites (collectively, the "Services").

Våld Labs is an independent music-software studio based in Lisbon, Portugal. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

Our apps and plugins

Our instruments and plugins — including TRESSE, Vekte, and others — run locally on your device. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your data. Your audio, presets, and projects are not sent to us as part of normal use.

Settings and presets you create are stored locally on your device or within your host application's project files. Where a product includes an optional online feature (such as checking for updates or validating a purchase), it contacts only the service needed for that feature and only when that feature is used.

In-app purchases

Some products offer a paid unlock (for example, the TRESSE "Pro" unlock). Purchases are processed entirely by the relevant platform store — Apple App Store or other distribution platforms. We never see or store your payment-card details. Those platforms handle the transaction under their own privacy policies. We may receive aggregate, non-identifying sales information from the store.

Website and waitlist

When you submit your email address to a waitlist or contact form on our websites, we use it solely to:

  • send you the release notes, updates, or replies you asked for; and
  • let you know about the specific product you signed up for.

We do not sell or rent your email address. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us at the address below.

Our websites are served as static pages and do not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Our hosting provider (Vercel) may process standard server log data, such as IP address and request information, for security and operational purposes.

Legal basis and your rights

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we process the limited personal data above on the basis of your consent (waitlist sign-up) and our legitimate interest in operating and securing the Services.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Data retention

We keep waitlist and contact emails only for as long as needed to provide the updates you requested, or until you ask us to remove them.

Children

Our Services are not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email vald.labs.lisbon@gmail.com.
