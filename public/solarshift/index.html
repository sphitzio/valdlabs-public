§ 01 / How it works

HOW IT WORKS.

In the shop
Looks like ink.
Prints like ink.

One premixed tub. No mixing, no dosing, no rituals. Set up your screen the way you already set up screens. Most shops are running their first test print inside an afternoon.

In the sun
Reveals full color in seconds.

UV in direct sunlight wakes the pigments up. The print shifts from a near-grayscale state to fully saturated color. The sun goes away, the print goes back. Forever, basically.

In the wash
Wears like a normal print.

Cures in standard ranges. Survives the dryer, the wash, the school year. Documented. Tested on garments we've been shipping ourselves for a decade.

§ 03 / Our story

WE'RE NOT A LAB.
WE'RE A PRINTER.

SolarShift wasn't born in a startup pitch deck. It was developed over years of refinement on our own production floor in Cancún — initially because we wanted shirts that did something nobody else's shirts did, and eventually because the ink turned out to be the most interesting thing we owned.

We've been screen-printing with this formula on our own line for ten years. We don't sell unproven chemistry. If the ink wasn't bulletproof, we'd have stopped using it years ago.

In 2026 we set up True Colors Trading LLC in Dubai to handle international licensing. SolarShift is now available to a small number of screen printers outside our own operation — in tiers, on terms, and with the documentation we wish we'd had ten years ago.

NERD STUFF

For the technical buyers who skip straight to specs.

01
Premixed in one tub

Single tub, single setup. No two-part mixing, no dosing windows, no chasing ratios on a deadline. The thing that ruins a Tuesday isn't the ink chemistry — it's the prep around it. We removed the prep.

02
Behaves like plastisol in your shop

Cures in standard plastisol ranges. Runs on the equipment you already own. We're not asking you to retool — we're asking you to swap a tub.

03
Documented for production

Cure windows, mesh recommendations, garment compatibility, wash protocol. All in writing, all from a shop that prints with this ink every day.

04
Wash-tested on garments we ship ourselves

We've been selling the printed garments for a decade. Wash data isn't a marketing claim — it's our own warranty exposure.

05
Infinite activation cycles

The reveal isn't a one-time effect. Sunlight, indoors, sunlight, indoors — same shirt, same print, hundreds of cycles deep. We've never measured a degradation point because we've never reached one.

06
IP-protected by design

We license access to the ink. We don't publish the formula and we don't sub-license. The license is what protects your investment in setting up around SolarShift — and what protects ours in spending ten years on it.
