Our Story

BORN UNDER
BLACKLIGHT

SolarShift started in a backroom print shop in 2019 — two screen printers obsessed with inks that did more. They wanted color that activated. Color that looked insane in daylight and went full nuclear under UV.

Two years of formula work later, the first jar of Shift Green hit the market. Within six months, three major streetwear labels and a festival merch house were printing exclusively in SolarShift. The rest, as they say, glowed.

Why SolarShift

ENGINEERED
FOR THE DROP

01
Ultra-Saturated Pigment

Our proprietary ChromaCore™ pigment system delivers 3× the color depth of standard plastisol inks. Blazing in daylight. Lethal under UV. No fading. No compromise.

02
UV Reactive Formula

Activates under UV light to shift from vivid to glowing — turning any print into a two-state experience. Ideal for festival merch, limited drops, and event apparel.

03
Wash-Stable Formula

Cured SolarShift inks are fully wash-stable, passing 50+ wash tests without color shift or glow degradation. Wears like standard plastisol, glows like nothing else.

04
Water-Based Option

All three colorways available in water-based formulation — OEKO-TEX compatible, better hand-feel on soft garments, and fully UV reactive.

05
Single-Pass Coverage

High-opacity formula covers even dark garments in a single pass on most mesh counts. Less flooding, more printing, better margins on your run.

06
Custom Mix Available

Need a specific shade in the SolarShift spectrum? Our lab offers custom ChromaCore blends for brands, artists, and large production runs.
